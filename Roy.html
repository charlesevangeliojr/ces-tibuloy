ICT Students at Dona Carmen Denia National High School

Information and Communications Technology (ICT) plays a crucial role in national development by driving economic growth, enhancing education, and improving healthcare. It helps businesses become more efficient and reach global markets, creating jobs and supporting economic progress. In education, ICT provides students and teachers with access to online resources and tools that enhance learning and skill development. In healthcare, ICT enables telemedicine, digital records, and better healthcare management, improving care quality and accessibility. It also connects people across distances, facilitating communication in both personal and professional contexts. Moreover, ICT drives innovation, enabling new technologies like AI and IoT while fostering entrepreneurship. Additionally, it helps bridge the digital divide by providing marginalized communities with access to education, information, and opportunities. Overall, ICT is essential for economic, social, and technological progress, playing a central role in modern nation-building.
